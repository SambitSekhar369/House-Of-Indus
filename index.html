About Us

Why We Do, What We Do

Back in 2009, my father gifted me a visual encyclopedia titled Our World, published by Paragon Books. From the towering Himalayas to the vast sands of the Sahara, from the ancient pyramids along the River Nile to the enigmatic Mayan ruins of Central America, from the bustling skyscrapers of modern metropolises to the pressing climate crisis affecting every corner of the globe—it was my gateway to the extraordinary stories of our planet. Unlike the routine of school, my real education came from the documentaries of National Geographic and Discovery. They showcased the world's wonders and ignited a lifelong passion in me.

House of Indus is born from this passion. We aim to inspire positive change by telling meaningful stories and empowering others to share theirs. Our mission spans history, nature, people, technology, culture and every other genre you could think of from all corners of the globe. Today, with technology bringing the world closer than ever, we strive to tell stories that you can experience from anywhere. Planet Earth is about experiences, and we want to bring them to you.

We also collaborate with institutions, organizations, and individuals across the globe to tell their stories and help build, enable and sustain their digital communities. Through our proven expertise in pre and post-production processes and storytelling frameworks, we give their voices the community they deserve.

Sambit

The Creator, House Of Indus
